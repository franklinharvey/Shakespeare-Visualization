Search Terms: Twelfth Night and She's The Man

I saw Twelfth Night once, probably around 2005. Not long after, a movie starring Amanda Bynes called She’s the Man came out (in 2006). This movie is a direct adaptation of Twelfth Night right down to the character names. The movie wasn’t remarkable, but it was actually my younger sister’s favorite movie for (way too many) years. I’ve seen it a dozen times (at least). I wanted to know how the searches were correlated the year the movie came out. There seems to be no correlation at all. My only explanation could be that She’s the Man’s audience was too young and didn’t care to make the connection.

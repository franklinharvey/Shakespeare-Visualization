Shakespeare’s modern cultural relevance can be hard to quantize. Surely most American’s who went to school have heard his name; most American high school graduates have surely read at least one play or sonnet by Shakespeare.

After watching Looking For Richard I was really tuned into the idea that most Americans have heard of Shakespeare, many have read his work, and few appreciate (or know how to appreciate) Shakespeare’s work. I have little influence in how the public perceives Shakespeare’s work, but I can do my best to display sentiment.

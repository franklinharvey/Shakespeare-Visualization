Search Terms: Richard III and House of Cards

I’m a huge fan of House of Cards. Most people who are (that I’ve talked to) love it for the great twists, conflicting feelings about characters, and beautifully crafted plots by complex characters. It’s not hard to see how House of Cards has been influenced by Richard III. I wanted to know if others had made the same connection. During the month of House of Cards’ initial release (March 2013) Richard III actually surpassed House of Cards as a search term. To me this makes sense. People would’ve discovered the show through Netflix and would take to Google to see it’s connection to Richard III.
